Chukwujekwu Joseph Ezema, also called Jeks, is originally from Enugu State, Nigeria. He is an accomplished and results-oriented Data Analyst with a proven track record of delivering impactful insights and driving business growth for over 4 years. He graduated with distinction in MSc Data Science from Teesside University, UK. Jeks brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the table.

Currently working as a Data Analyst at Ubisoft Reflections in Newcastle, UK. He equally leverages his advanced skills in Excel, Power BI, Tableau, JavaScript, Python, SQL, and R—to champion data-driven training resources at Jeks Analytics Platform. With a keen eye for details and a passion for innovation, he thrives in dynamic environments, delivering actionable solutions that fuel strategic initiatives and propel organisations forward. From his YouTube engagements and community supports, he has mentored over 300 people who're transitioning into tech.

He also takes pride in his over 2.5 years of experience in the financial sector, gained at Page Financials in Lagos, Nigeria. As a Credit Analyst, he honed his customer relationship skills, problem-solving abilities, and analytical proficiency using Advanced Excel and CRM tools. His exceptional performance in Consumer Lending led him to be transferred to support functions as a Data Analyst 10 months before embarking on his master's in the UK.

During his masters studies, Jeks demonstrated his commitment to academic excellence and community engagement by volunteering as a PASS Leader in MSc Data Science and serving as an Accommodation Buddy. His dedication to supporting his peers earned him a Silver Award for surpassing 75 hours of volunteer service. Additionally, he has contributed as a research author to an unpublished paper that is currently under review by journal publishers, showcasing his passion for advancing knowledge and contributing to the academic community.

Prior to his experiences above, Jeks had excelled as a Country Manager and Business Consultant at Fashion Hometex Co. Ltd. Thailand, where he spearheaded market research efforts, developed comprehensive business strategies, and established successful Nigerian marketing and sales models for the company. His ability to blend analytical prowess with strategic thinking has consistently delivered tangible results and positioned him as a trusted advisor in the industry.

With a commitment to continuous learning and professional development, Jeks remains at the forefront of emerging trends and technologies in AI, Data Science and Analytics. His relentless pursuit of excellence and dedication to driving business impact, make him a valuable asset to work with.
