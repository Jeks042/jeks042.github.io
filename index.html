About Jeks

Chukwujekwu Joseph Ezema, also called Jeks, is an accomplished and results-oriented Data Analyst with a proven track record of delivering impactful insights and driving business growth. He graduated with distinction in MSc Data Science from Teesside University, UK. Jeks brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the table.

Currently working as a Data Analyst at Ubisoft Reflections in Newcastle, UK, Jeks leverages his advanced skills in Excel, Power BI, Tableau, JavaScript, Python, SQL, and R to drive data-driven decision-making and enhance operational efficiency. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for innovation, he thrives in dynamic environments, delivering actionable insights that fuel strategic initiatives and propel organisations forward. From a single unit, this has fetched him to manage studio-wide data analytics requests from other teams in Ubisoft. He also takes pride in his over 2.5 years of experience in the financial sector, gained at Page Financials in Lagos, Nigeria. As a Credit Analyst, he honed his customer relationship skills, problem-solving abilities, and analytical proficiency using Advanced Excel and CRM tools. His exceptional performance in Consumer Lending led to a transition to support functions as a Transaction Analyst three months before embarking on his masters in the UK.

During his masters studies, Jeks demonstrated his commitment to academic excellence and community engagement by volunteering as a PASS Leader in MSc Data Science and serving as an Accommodation Buddy. His dedication to supporting his peers earned him a Silver Award for surpassing 75 hours of volunteer service. Additionally, he contributed as a research author to an unpublished paper that is currently in draft, showcasing his passion for advancing knowledge and contributing to the academic community.

Prior to his roles above, Jeks excelled as a Country Manager and Business Consultant at Fashion Hometex Co. Ltd. Thailand, where he spearheaded market research efforts, developed comprehensive business strategies, and established successful Nigerian marketing and sales models for the company. His ability to blend analytical prowess with strategic thinking has consistently delivered tangible results and positioned him as a trusted advisor in the industry.

With a commitment to continuous learning and professional development, Jeks remains at the forefront of emerging trends and technologies in AI, Data Science and Analytics. His relentless pursuit of excellence and dedication to driving business impact make him a valuable asset.
